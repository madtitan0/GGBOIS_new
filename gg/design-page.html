A revolutionary wearable device to track brain activity, vital signs, and motor recovery in comatose and brain-damaged patients.

What does ReAwaken do?

"By integrating advanced sensor technology, machine learning, and real-time data reporting, ReAwaken aims to provide healthcare professionals with critical insights into patient conditions, ultimately improving recovery outcomes."

Vital Signs Monitoring: Track essential metrics.

Brain Activity Measurement: Utilize EEG sensors to monitor.

RealTime Reporting: Provide immediate feedback to healthcare.

Comfort and Portability: Designed for long-term wear.

Our Motives

Our Motives

Empower Patient Recovery

We aim to enhance the recovery process for comatose and brain-damaged patients by providing healthcare professionals with real-time, actionable data. Our device is designed to facilitate timely interventions that can significantly improve patient outcomes.

Innovate Healthcare Technology

At ReAwaken, we believe in the power of innovation. We are committed to integrating cutting-edge technology, such as machine learning and advanced sensors, to create a device that not only monitors vital signs but also provides intelligent insights into patient health.

Enhance Quality of Care

Our goal is to support healthcare providers in delivering the highest quality of care. By equipping them with the tools they need to monitor and analyze patient data effectively, we strive to improve overall treatment strategies and recovery rates.

Promote Accessibility

We are dedicated to making our technology accessible to all patients and healthcare providers, regardless of their location or resources. We aim to bridge gaps in healthcare access, ensuring that every patient receives the monitoring and support they need.

Foster Collaboration

We believe in the importance of collaboration among healthcare professionals, researchers, and families. By creating a platform that encourages communication and data sharing, we aim to foster a community dedicated to improving patient care.

Drive Continuous Improvement

At ReAwaken, we are committed to continuous learning and improvement. We actively seek feedback from users—patients, caregivers, and healthcare providers—to refine our device and ensure it meets the evolving needs of those we serve.

Design for Any Device

Technologies used

EEG Monitoring (Electroencephalography)

Monitor brain wave patterns and detect signs of consciousness, seizures, or abnormal brain activity.

EMG Monitoring (Electromyography)

Track muscle activity to detect signs of motor recovery or reflexes in patients.

ECG/BP (Electrocardiography & Blood Pressure)

Continuously measure heart rate and blood pressure to monitor cardiovascular health.

fNIRS (Functional Near-Infrared Spectroscopy)

Monitor brain oxygenation and blood flow to assess brain function and neural recovery.

ICP Monitoring (Intracranial Pressure)

Track intracranial pressure to detect dangerous brain swelling and ensure brain health.

FAQ

How does ReAwaken monitor brain activity?

ReAwaken uses EEG and fNIRS sensors to monitor brain wave patterns and cerebral oxygenation, providing insights into the brain’s activity levels.

Is ReAwaken comfortable for long-term wear?

Yes, the device is designed to be lightweight and comfortable, allowing for continuous monitoring without discomfort.

Who can use ReAwaken?

ReAwaken is designed for comatose and brain-damaged patients in hospital, rehabilitation centers, or home settings.

How does ReAwaken provide treatment recommendations?

Our AI analyzes patient data and uses pattern recognition and machine learning to suggest personalized treatment options based on recovery trends.
